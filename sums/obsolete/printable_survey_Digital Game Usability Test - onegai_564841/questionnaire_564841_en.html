Digital Game Usability Test - onegai

The present study is developed to obtain a Master's in Digital Game Development at the Department of Communication and Art from the  University of Aveiro.

 

This study regards the development of the digital game "onegai" and its playable character, a non-identified OLD MAN.

 

The study participation involves two steps:

1) Playing the digital game for 35 minutes;

2) Answering and submitting the present questionnaire.

 

Before advancing on this survey, click here to download the uploaded package that contains the game prototype, suitable for Windows.

After the download, look for the folder location and unzip it.

Finally, click on the application file (with the .exe extension) to start playing.

 

The questionnaire consists of five parts and 25 questions, during approximately 20 minutes.

The data collected is anonymous and confidential, under the responsibility of the master's student, Anthony Pereira (anthonypereira@ua.pt).

There are no wrong or right answers. Your participation is essential, and your effort is appreciated. 

There are 28 questions in this survey.
